We are excited about the idea of a digital keyboard that could potentially replace physical peripherals because we believe that such a keyboard, if implemented correctly, could not only boost productivity but also drastically reduce electronic waste. With a physical keyboard there is always the risk of small things breaking and rendering the entire device useless, causing you to be forced to buy another one and throw away the old one, this is how a digital keyboard could reduce waste and help save our planet. Additionaly, a digital keyboard could boost productivity especially when used with things such as voice recognition since this techonlogy has been around for a long time now but isnt being used to its full potential.
